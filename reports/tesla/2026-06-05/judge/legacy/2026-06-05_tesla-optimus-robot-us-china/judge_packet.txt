Why this node:
- For a humanoid ramp, the most “physical” gating function is often not one SKU but the ability to produce high-DOF joint modules with consistent reliability at cost.
- It is a **bottleneck** hypothesis until proven by lead times/backlog/capex/yield limits; it is not assumed to be a chokepoint.

Substitution/escape hatches to watch:
- Direct-drive or alternative actuation architectures that reduce dependence on precision reducers/ball screws.
- Tesla vertical integration of joint modules (reduces merchant supplier value capture).
